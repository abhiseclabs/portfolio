About Me

I'm Abhishek Sharma, a cybersecurity professional in the making, pursuing an M.S. in Cybersecurity at Yeshiva University's Katz School and CompTIA Security+ certified. My path started personally. Watching someone close to me get phished showed me how vulnerable everyday people are online, and sparked a drive to do something about it. I bring 8+ years of quality control, compliance, and audit experience from manufacturing, including supervising and training staff and earning a Lean Six Sigma Green Belt, which shaped the process discipline and attention to detail I now bring to security. That same drive led me to a Salesforce Certified Platform Administrator certification and additional networking and security coursework outside my degree. I've built hands-on projects like an AI-powered phishing email analyzer, mentor family and friends on account security, and hold a diploma in photography, where that same eye for detail carries into my security work. I'm actively seeking cybersecurity internship opportunities to bring that same rigor to real security problems.
